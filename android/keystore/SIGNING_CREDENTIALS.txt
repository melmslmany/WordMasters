IMPORTANT — Play Console upload key
-----------------------------------
PureApp v1 on Play was signed with a DIFFERENT certificate.
Until you reset the upload key, Play will reject new builds.

1. Play Console → com.emexp.PureApp → Setup → App signing
2. Request upload key reset
3. Upload: android/keystore/upload_certificate.pem
4. After Google approves (1-2 days), upload the AAB again.

Also enroll in Play App Signing if prompted (required for AAB).

• Keep this file backed up with the .jks file.
• After first Play upload, Google Play App Signing may enroll this as upload key.
• If Play rejects signature, request "Upload key reset" in Play Console
  (Setup → App signing) and upload the certificate from this keystore.

Export certificate (for Play upload key reset):
  keytool -export -rfc -alias wordmasters -file upload_certificate.pem -keystore word-masters-upload.jks
