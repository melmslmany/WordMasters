Privacy Policy (English)

Word Masters (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is a word puzzle game published under the package name com.wordsearch.WordMasters.

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app may collect, how it is used, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Game progress — levels completed, scores, settings, and streaks stored locally on your device and optionally synced if you sign in.
  • Account data — if you choose Google Sign-In: display name, email, and profile photo via Firebase Authentication.
  • Analytics — Firebase Analytics may collect anonymous usage events (screens, sessions, device model, OS version) to improve the game.
  • Advertising — Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and usage data to show and measure ads. You may see personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your region and consent choices.
  • Crash & performance — technical logs to keep the app stable.

How we use information

  • Provide gameplay, save progress, and sync across devices when signed in.
  • Show advertisements and in-app rewards.
  • Improve features, fix bugs, and understand aggregate usage.

Third-party services

The app uses:

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Analytics)
  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Sign-In (optional login)

Each service has its own privacy policy. See Google Privacy Policy.

Children

Word Masters is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Contact us to request deletion.

Your rights

Depending on your region (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. You can clear local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage. For account-linked data, contact us below.
