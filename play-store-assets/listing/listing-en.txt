Word Masters — The Ultimate Word Puzzle Experience

Train your brain with two exciting game modes in one beautiful neon-dark app:

🎯 CLASSIC WORD SEARCH
Drag across the grid to find hidden words — horizontal, vertical, and diagonal. Categories include Animals, Food, Nature, Sports, Colors, Countries, and many more. Each category offers up to 500 levels!

🔤 WORD GUESS
Tap glowing hex letter tiles to spell words and complete the level. Shuffle, undo, reset, and use hints when you need a boost.

✨ WHY PLAYERS LOVE WORD MASTERS
• 20+ themed categories with hundreds of levels
• Two unique play styles — search & spell
• Daily tasks and login rewards — earn coins every day
• Hint system: reveal a word or a letter

• 11 UI languages: English, Arabic, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Turkish, Russian, Portuguese, Hindi, Indonesian
• Stunning dark neon design — easy on the eyes, fun to play
• Progress saved automatically — pick up where you left off
